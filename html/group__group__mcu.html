◆ mcu_initClocks()

void mcu_initClocks ( void  )

Initializes microcontroller clocks.

The final MCU clock frequency is computed as f_CPU=f_XTAL*(MulInt+MulFrac)/PllDiv . The parameters MulInt, MulFrac and PllDiv are defined by macros.
